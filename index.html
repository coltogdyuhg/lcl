What We Do

Our Services

🌿

Grass Cutting

Clean, even cuts every time. We trim to the right height for your grass type and leave your lawn looking sharp — no missed strips, no scalping.

⚡

Weed Whacking

We get into every tight spot — fence lines, tree bases, curb edges — anywhere the mower can't reach. Your borders will be crisp and clean.

🍂

Leaf Pickup

Fall messes don't stand a chance. We rake, blow, and haul away leaves so your yard is clear and your grass can breathe heading into winter.

🗑️

Trash & Junk Hauling

Got junk piling up in the yard or by the curb? We load it and take it away. Old furniture, yard waste, debris — gone same day.

✂️

Edging

A sharp edge along your driveway or sidewalk makes the whole yard pop. We finish every job with clean lines that frame the work.

🧹

Full Yard Cleanup

Moving in, moving out, or just need a fresh start? Our full cleanup service takes your yard from overgrown and neglected to completely dialed in.

🪣

Trash Can Cleaning

Stinky, grimy trash cans are a problem nobody wants to deal with — so we handle it for you. We scrub, sanitize, and deodorize your bins inside and out so they stop smelling and stay fresh. Add it to any service visit or book it on its own.
